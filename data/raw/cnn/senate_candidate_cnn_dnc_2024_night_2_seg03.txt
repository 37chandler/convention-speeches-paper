SENATE CANDIDATE ():

Good evening, America. And, hello, Maryland! People like me -- thank you. People like me, stories like mine, don't usually make it to the United States Senate, but they should. I am the proud granddaughter of a housekeeper, Sarah Daisy (ph), who raised her three children in a one-bedroom apartment. It was her dream to work in government, to help people. That meant taking a typing test, but she didn't know how to type and she couldn't afford a typewriter. So, she put a white piece of paper on the refrigerator and drew a keyboard on it. And every night she stood in front of that refrigerator and taught herself to type.
