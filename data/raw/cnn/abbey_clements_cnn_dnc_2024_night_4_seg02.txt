ABBEY CLEMENTS (FORMER SANDY HOOK ELEMENTARY TEACHER):

On December 14, 2012, I walked into Sandy Hook School. I stopped at the office, chatted with my principal, then started my day with my second graders. Suddenly, a loud crash, like metal folding chairs falling, one hundred fifty four gunshots blaring. Hiding in the coats, trying to sing with my students, trying to read to them, trying to drown out the sounds, terror, crying, running, I carry that horrific day with me. Twenty beautiful first grade children and six of my beautiful colleagues were killed. They should still be here. KIMBERLY MATA-RUBIO,
