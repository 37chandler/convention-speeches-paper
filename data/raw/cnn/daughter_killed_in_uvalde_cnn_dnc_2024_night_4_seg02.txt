DAUGHTER KILLED IN UVALDE (TEXAS SCHOOL SHOOTING):

It's 10:30 AM at Robb Elementary in Uvalde, the school is recognizing my 10-year-old daughter, Lexi, for receiving all As. She receives a good citizen award, and we pose for photos. She wears a St. Mary sweatshirt and a smile that lights up the room. Thirsty minutes later, a gunman murders her, eighteen classmates and two teachers. We are taken to a private room where police tell us she isn't coming home. Uvalde is national news. Parents everywhere reach for their children. I reach out for the daughter I will never hold again.
