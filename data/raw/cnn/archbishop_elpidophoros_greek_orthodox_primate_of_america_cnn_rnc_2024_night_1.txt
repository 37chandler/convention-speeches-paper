ARCHBISHOP ELPIDOPHOROS GREEK ORTHODOX PRIMATE OF AMERICA ():

Let us pray to the Lord of all, almighty and eternal God, in peace we come before you and implore your infinite goodness, look upon this gathering of our nation's political leaders and grant them illuminant hearts and minds to serve their fellow citizens through this Republican National Convention. Grant and to the nominee, President Donald J. Trump, and his advisors and counselors, wisdom, grace, and the spirit of duty that they may embrace all Americans' needs, shining the light on good governance upon the venerable institutions of the world's best hope for everyone's life, liberty, and pursuit of happiness. Grant unto the delegates a powerful recognition of purpose to bear witness to divine providence, which has shielded this great land through the centuries, and drives us to a more perfect union with one another, ever building on the labors of those pioneers of patriotism, who went before us. Preserve, protect and defend the servicemen and women who daily safeguard our freedoms, so that in prosperity and tranquility, we can worship you the creator, redeemer in sanctifier of the universe, and be the blessed people whose God is the Lord. Amen.
